I took a handful of great classes at Stanford covering related subjects: “History of Capitalism” with Jennifer Burns, “American Economic History” with Gavin Wright, “The Relations Between Philosophical Enquiry and Historical Enquiry” with Rahul Chaudhri, and “Capitalism and its Discontents” with Sean Hanretta and J.P. Daughton. I read Niall Ferguson, Jonathan Levy, Albert Hirschman and Emma Rothschild books in my free time.
